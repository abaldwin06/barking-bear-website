In Home Assessment & Coaching

Private in-home or virtual sessions allow first-hand observation and diagnosis of problem behaviors, and an opportunity for personalized hands-on coaching of the humans so that you can understand, manage, or modify your dog's behavior.

One on One Skills Sessions

Anne works one-on-one training your dog in your home, at her home, or out in the world to build the foundation for new behaviors. These focused, consistent sessions give your dog a head start in learning so your training is more streamlined and effective at home.

Puppy Fresh Start Program

Every day of a puppy's first months is a learning opportunity that will last a lifetime. The puppy fresh start program is a series of 1:1 sessions to make the most of that time window. You get a break from that puppy energy and Anne takes your puppy out to get that crucial socialization and learning in.
