In-Home Dog Boarding

Family Style Dog Boarding

A mountain getaway for your pup with all the comforts of home

Barking Bear Dog Boarding takes place in Anne & Dan's family home in Kittredge, CO, 30-45 min from Denver, which has:

  • A fully fenced acre for playtime & potty breaks
  • Ten acres of meadow & forest for hikes and zoomies.
  • Two dog-proofed bedrooms dedicated to canine guests.
  • A "rumble room" for play time with couches for naps.
  • Pet gated sections of the house to give dogs their own space until they are fully comfortable with one other.

Professional, insured pet sitters who treat your pup like family

The Barking Bear family is made up of:

  • Two dog behavior savvy, licensed, insured, and professional pet sitters, Dan & Anne, who both care for your pet and work from home.
  • Our two rescue pups, Ullie and Riley, and a rotating adoptable foster dog.
  • Two cats in their golden years, who are kept separate from all dogs in the house.
  • A wonderful community of long-time customers, so your pup will see familiar friendly faces every stay.

We can accommodate a wide range of temperaments and special needs with Standard Boarding and VIP Boarding*

  • Vaccinated and licensed dogs of any breed, age, or size are welcome
  • Crate trained dogs are eligible for standard boarding and *VIP kennel-free guests should be non-destructive and able to relax in a room by themselves*
  • *All puppies under 1 year are VIP guests* and adults should be spayed/neutered
  • Dog social, dog selective, dog tolerant, and *and VIP guests that need full separation from other dogs* are welcome, if tolerant of dogs under the same roof
  • Guests who need meds, enrichment routines, or elaborate food preparation are welcomed. On a case by case basis, *some special needs may require VIP boarding*
  • Dogs with fear, anxiety, reactivity, and separation anxiety* are welcome after a successful trial overnight. Private training, daycare, and additional trial overnights may be recommended to make these guests comfortable.
  • Pet parents with "separation anxiety", you will get frequent photo and video updates with either boarding option!
