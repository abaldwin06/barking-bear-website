Meet your trainer

Trainer Anne Baldwin

Dedicated to evidence-backed, fear-free training techniques

I use positive reinforcement techniques because they are proven to be the most effective and ethical. Not just in books and statistics, but in real life with real dogs and people. I am continually expanding my knowledge and skills, so you can trust you have expert in your corner.

Meeting you where you're at with empathy & compassion

I'm not here to judge or criticize. Like any dog owner, I've made mis-steps and I've learned from every one of them. I'm here to listen with kindness and compassion, and guide you with expertise and optimism every step of the way.

Customizing a training plan to match you and your dog

I provide clear, easy to follow guidance catered to your preferred learning style using videos, writing, and progress tracking. I combine training sessions where I coach the human with training sessions one-on-one with your dog so that your dog's skills get a jump start that you can run with.

In Home Assessment & Coaching

In-Home & Virtual Sessions

Per 75min session: $150
Package of 3: $375 ($75 savings!)
Package of 5: $600 ($150 savings!)

Best option to address of problem behaviors in the home

In these sessions, you and your dog work with Anne in your home or virtually to:

  • Identify root causes of your dog's behaviors in the context where they occur
  • Learn and practice behavior modification exercises with your dog to continue after the session
  • Discuss behavior management techniques and environmental changes to prevent and mitigate problem behaviors
  • Come to understand the "why" behind both your dog's behavior and how best to advocate for their well-being and live harmoniously together
  • Mitigate behaviors like overarousal, jumping, leash pulling, impolite dog-dog interactions, nuisance barking, resource guarding, house soiling, and more

Private Dog Training Sessions For The Dogs

One on One Skills Sessions

Best option to accelerate progress and solidify new behaviors

In these sessions, your attendance is optional! Anne and your dog work one-on-one in your home or elsewhere to:

  • Get extra practice learning new skills, manners, or behaviors - without extra time and effort from you
  • Guide your dog through the initial learning process so you just have to maintain the new behaviors, not teach them from the ground up
  • Great for learning basic obedience & tricks, confidence building, rehearsing recall and leash skills, practicing polite greetings, perfecting patio and brewery manners and getting extra repetitions in of any behavior modification training
  • This is a great option to add on to In Home Coaching to help accelerate your dog's learning progress

Private Dog Training Sessions For The Puppies

Puppy Fresh Start Program

In-Home & Field Trip Sessions

Per 75min session: $150
Package of 3: $375 ($75 savings!)
Package of 5: $600 ($150 savings!)

Best option for puppies under 1 year of age

  • Help your pup grow up confidently in this exciting, overwhelming, and sometimes scary world!
  • The best time for puppy socialization is 3-16 weeks, but the second best time is NOW! Make the most of puppyhood and set your pup up for lifelong success with positive socialization and confidence building sessions, done by a professional
  • We'll craft a personalized plan to meet your goals and match your puppy's personality, combining in-home sessions and field trips
  • Don't wait until your pup is fully vaccinated to start! Sessions can be in the safety of your home and field trips can involve safely observing the world from a carrier
  • Great for learning basic obedience, practicing polite greetings of humans and dogs, gentle exposure and desensitization to common "scary" sights and sounds, reinforcing crate and potty training, and getting some of your puppy's crazy energy out while you get a break!
  • Recommend 3 sessions per week to maximize learning during this life stage
