Dog Boarding

Family Style Dog Boarding

Barking Bear Boarding takes place in our home, just 30 minutes outside of Denver.

  • We have a fully fenced acre for playtime & potty breaks.
  • We have 10 acres of meadow & forest for hikes and zoomies.
  • Dan & Anne both will care for your pup and we both work from home. So your dog will rarely, if ever, be home alone.
  • We welcome crate trained OR non-destructive canine guests.
  • With dog social and dog selective pups, we do slow dog-dog introductions and gradually integrate guests into our pack.
  • Dogs that need to be separate from other dogs are welcome if they can tolerate the sounds and smells of other dogs.
  • We have two cats, but they live in our bedroom and are kept fully separate from all dogs in the house.
  • We foster dog social rescue pups, but we only introduce them to guests after their adjustment period.
