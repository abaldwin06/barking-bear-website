About Us

Meet Anne & Bear

My name is Anne, and this is Mister Bear - the inspiration for Barking Bear Pet Care.

Bear has since passed over the rainbow bridge, but he was a very special and sensitive old man. Finding someone I could trust with my best friend when I traveled was incredibly difficult - he ran away from home with pet sitters more than once. I want to be the kind of pet caretaker that Bear and I needed.

We are pet sitters who:

  • Understand how important your pet is to you
  • Appreciate your pet's unique personality and needs
  • Give parents peace of mind and keep them up to date
  • Will never use aversive training tools or methods on your pet
  • Treat your pet like part of our family while you're away

About Boarding

About Our Boarding Services

Barking Bear Boarding takes place in our Denver Highlands home.

  • We have a yard with a 6 foot privacy fence for playtime.
  • Both Dan and Anne will be caring for your pup.
  • Our guests can be either crate trained OR non-destructive.
  • Your pup won't be alone often, Dan & Anne work from home full and part time, respectively.
  • Our guests do not have to be dog social, but able to coexist with our pack after a slow introduction.
  • We have two cats, but they live in our bedroom and are kept fully out of sight and separate from all dogs in the house.
  • We rarely take boarding clients from more than one family at a time, please let us know if your pup would do best as the only boarder.
  • We foster dog social rescue pups, and we don't take boarding clients during the foster adjustment period.
