Why choose Barking Bear?

Barking Bear guests are treated like family and appreciated for their own unique personalities. Your pup will get personalized, professional care while enjoying a mountain oasis full of smells and room to run.

Comfortable Family Environment

We limit number of pups we care for so that we can integrate them into our day to day like family. We are meticulous with dog-dog introductions and cleanliness in order to maintain comfort & safety.

Fear Free Dog Training

We have years of experience using positive reinforcement training personally and professionally and are proud to have helped many dogs and their people overcome behavioral challenges and adolescent exuberance.

Enrichment and Adventure

We offer an ideal environment for meeting dogs' physical and emotional needs with hikes, playtime, and learning. Treat your dog to their own vacation or to weekly fun and you'll have a tired & happy pup.

Dog Boarding

About Our Dog Boarding Services

Barking Bear Boarding takes place in our home, just 30 minutes outside of Denver.

  • We have just under one acre of meadow fully fenced for playtime, potty breaks, and sunbathing.
  • We have ten acres of private land for hikes, sniffari's, and off leash zoomies for pups with reliable recall.
  • Both Dan and Anne will be both be caring for your pup.
  • Your pup won't be alone often, Dan & Anne work from home full and part time, respectively.
  • We welcome guests who are either crate trained OR non-destructive.
  • Our guests do not have to be dog social, but able to coexist with our pack after a slow introduction.
  • We have two cats, but they live in our bedroom and are kept fully separate from all dogs in the house.
  • We foster dog social rescue pups, and we don't take boarding clients during the foster adjustment period.

About Us

Meet Anne & Bear

My name is Anne, and this is Mister Bear - the inspiration for Barking Bear Pet Care.

Bear has since passed over the rainbow bridge, but he was a very special and sensitive old man. Finding someone I could trust with my best friend when I traveled was incredibly difficult, and finding a training approach that worked for both of us was even harder. I want to be the kind of pet caretaker and training support that Bear and I needed.

We are pet sitters and trainers who:

  • Understand how important your pet is to you
  • Appreciate your pet's unique personality and needs
  • Empathize with the individuals at both ends of the leash
  • Give parents peace of mind and keep them up to date
  • Will never use aversive training tools or methods on your pet
  • Treat your pet like part of our family while you're away

Our Team

The Barking Bear Family

Anne

Chief Dog Wrangler

Anne has fostered countless dogs over the years and spent the last few years transitioning into a dog training career. She loves teaching tricks, agility, and fundamentals, and she's especially passionate about helping dogs with big feelings (fearfulness or reactivity) navigate the world.

Dan

Chief Dog Cuddler

A lifelong dog lover, Dan is excited to finally have a little pack of his own. He's become quite adept at winning over dogs that are fearful of men and he expertly prepares (separate) meals for dogs and humans alike. Scritches and snuggles are also some of his specialties.

Riley

a.k.a. The Boss

Riley carefully vets every new dog to come into her home, but once they're on her good side she'll lead them fearlessly on walks, hikes, and other adventures.
