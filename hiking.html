Barking Bear Group Dog Hikes

Our group dog hikes are designed to optimize your dog's individual needs

  • Fewer dogs per handler to ensure individualized attention, slow dog-dog introductions, and safety.
  • Longer leashes to reduce tension between dogs and ensure room to sniff, explore, and go your own pace.
  • Greater focus on meeting your dog's needs. Not just exercise, but sniffing, learning, and positive socialization.
  • Hikes are lead by a trainer to maintain and reinforce polite hiking behaviors and accommodate fearful or reactive dogs.
  • Week to week consistency. We are a husband & wife team with amazing & loyal clients, not a rotating cast of characters.

Our pack hikes occur Thursdays with pickup between 7-745am and dropoff between 1130-1230pm. To join the Thursday crew or get on the wait list for other days, click here.

Training Hike

$130

90 minutes of hiking & training
One on one with a trainer
Pickup & dropoff included!

You only have so much time to enjoy the outdoors with your pup, so let us help you skip to the good part!

Outdoors hiking skills we will work on with your pup:

  • Manners for passing dogs, hikers, & bikers. Especially important for reactive, fearful or anxious dogs to practice.
  • Recall & Off-Leash fundamentals to get you ready for that next camping trip or off leash trail you've been eyeing!
  • Neutrality around wildlife using cutting edge predation substitution training protocols.
  • Reduce leash pulling, especially on the downhill, to ensure fun and safety for all species on your next hike.
  • Using only force free and ethical training practices. We will never use aversive equipment or punishment.

Depending on your pup's learning pace, you can expect noticable progress on skills over 5 to 10 hikes plus a private lesson to transfer skills to you!
